Shop - Earn - Share

iRICH is a unique membership-based system of bringing together businesses and consumers in a way that is mutually benificial.

iRICH is a single line system that helps its suppliers grow their businesses, helps consumers earn while they spend and let's all members benifit financially.

iRICH benifits small business owners and consumers alike!

Business Member

• Customer loyalty and grow your extra income during your regular business.

• An ongoing and reliable database of prospects and clients who are local and genuinely interested in your services.

• A way for you to earn more than just the revenue generated from sales to clients, in the form of a share in the iRICH pool.

Individual Member

For busy individuals who need reliable everyday services

• Customer can choose an ongoing iRICH suppliers nearby like restaurants, grocery stores, fuel stations, hairdressers, travel agents etc.

• A way for you to earn back your spend, in the form of a share in the iRICH pool - your receive a share of this pool at the end of each monthe. More the spend and more the members you introduce, the more you earn.

• An easy and convenient way to manage your personal budgets and cashflows. The iRICH App provides you a convenient easy to use platform to bring your expenditure management into one place
